# Free and Open-Source Resources for 3D Models, Textures, Sounds, and Animations

## 3D Models
- **[Sketchfab](https://sketchfab.com/)** - A platform that offers a wide range of free 3D models, many of which are open-source.
- **[TurboSquid](https://www.turbosquid.com/)** - Offers a selection of free 3D models under various licenses.
- **[Free3D](https://free3d.com/)** - A repository of free 3D models available for download.

## Textures
- **[CC0 Textures](https://cc0textures.com/)** - A collection of public domain textures available for any use.
- **[Texture Haven](https://texturehaven.com/)** - Offers quality textures under the CC0 license.
- **[Textures.com](https://www.textures.com/)** - Free textures available for various needs.

## Sounds
- **[Freesound](https://freesound.org/)** - A collaborative database of Creative Commons licensed sounds.
- **[Zapsplat](https://www.zapsplat.com/)** - A collection of free sound effects and music tracks.
- **[Sounds Resource](https://www.sounds-resource.com/)** - Archive of sound samples from various sources.

## Animations
- **[Mixamo](https://www.mixamo.com/)** - Provides 3D character animations that can be applied to your own models.
- **[Animation Resources](https://animationresources.org/)** - Offers various resources related to animation, including free clips and tutorials.
- **[OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/)** - A site for open-source graphics, including animations for games.
