# Development Roadmap for The Great War of Land

## Phase 1: Conceptualization (January 2026 - February 2026)
- Define the core game concept and mechanics.
- Research and gather visual references.
- Create initial sketches and prototype designs.

## Phase 2: Pre-production (March 2026 - June 2026)
- Develop a Design Document outlining gameplay mechanics, story, and art direction.
- Assemble a development team and assign roles.
- Create a detailed project timeline.

## Phase 3: Production (July 2026 - December 2027)
- Begin asset creation: models, animations, textures.
- Develop the game's systems and mechanics in Unreal Engine 5.
- Regular playtesting to refine gameplay.

## Phase 4: Alpha Testing (January 2028 - March 2028)
- Implement all game features.
- Begin closed alpha testing with select players.
- Collect feedback and make necessary adjustments.

## Phase 5: Beta Testing (April 2028 - June 2028)
- Open beta testing to a wider audience.
- Focus on bug fixing and performance optimization.
- Finalize marketing strategies leading to launch.

## Phase 6: Launch (July 2028)
- Release the game to the public.
- Monitor player feedback and support post-launch updates and patches.

## Phase 7: Post-Launch Support (August 2028 - Ongoing)
- Continue to engage with the community.
- Release additional content and updates based on player feedback and engagement.
